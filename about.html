Welcome!

We are a local Colorado restaurant that specializes in classic Mediterranean food. We started this journey 25 years ago, from the very beginning our goal was to introduce others to gourmet food from Greece. It has always been a dream of ours to open a Greek restaurant here in the United States. Our restaurant is a family business and it has been run throughout the years by several generations. We are looking forward to the future and our plan is to open a few more locations in Colorado so that we can continue to make delicious Greek food for as many people as possible.

All of our food is prepared daily by our experienced chefs. We are also proud to say that we source all of our meat and produce from the Denver area. Our main goal is to serve our guests authentic, tasty, Greek cuisine.

Come try our Mediterranean cuisine, we are located in the Denver Tech Center!
